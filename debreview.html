CLAUDE DEBUSSY, MAURICE RAVEL

Dr. [Forte] performs this great piano repertoire beautifully. She has all the technique necessary to project every nuance with subtle qualities that let the music speak for itself. The listener is never overwhelmed with virtuosity but feels there is always plenty in reserve if more is needed.

[Forte's] playing emphasizes long lines supported by fluent rhythms. she obviously knows this repertoire inside-out and has probably played it for many years. At least, her playing suggests this to this reviewer.

If you are looking for superb French piano music played beautifully, this CD should be your "cup of tea." I suspect both Debussy and Ravel would like this recording.

Maurice Hinson
Journal of the American Liszt Society
v.40 / July–Dec. 1996
