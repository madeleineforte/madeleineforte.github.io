French-born pianist Madeleine Forte has enchanted audiences on five continents with her performances of Ravel, Debussy, Chopin and others. She maintains a busy international concert and recording schedule, writes on musical issues and teaches masterclasses all over the world.

Please take a stroll through this site for more information on Madeleine Forte. To listen to excerpts from her recordings, click here.
